Cinnamon Rolls

What you need:

  • 1 roll of pre-made puff pastry
  • 100 gram Sugar
  • 25-40 grams of Cinnamon powder
  • Baking Sheet with Baking Paper
  • Oven
  • knife

What to do?

  1. The step before all steps, pre-heat your oven to 180 degrees celcius. It can take between 10 and 20 minutes for your oven to heat up.
  2. As the oven is heating up, prepare your ingredients, make sure you have everything measured out and ready to use.
  3. Your puff pastry might be a little hard to use when it is still cold, don't worry, when it gets to room temperature it will get easier to use.
  4. Roll out the puff pastry on a flat, clean surface
  5. Mix the sugar and cinnamon well with a spoon.
  6. Spread the sugar and cinnamon mixture over the dough. Make sure that you leave about 3 centimeters at the bottom empty.
  7. Starting from the top, roll up the dough with the sugar mixture inside.
  8. Using a bit of water, wet the bottom of the dough to seal it shut.
  9. Using a big, sharp knife, but the big roll into even pieces, about 2cm thick.
  10. Place the rolls on the baking paper, swirl side up. Make sure to leave a little space between the rolls.
  11. That was most of the hard work! Put them in oven once it has reached the right temperature.
  12. Bake them for 18-20 minutes. You want them to be nice and golden brown, pay special attention that the inside is also done cooking.
  13. Leave them too cool, It is incredibly tempting to eat them right away, but you will burn your mouth!

Extra info

Don't let outher people tell you that you are cheating by using pre-made puff pastry. It is a pain to make, and we are looking for easy treats, not problems.
You can use whatever kind of suger you like, as long as it is not sugar cubes. Try and experiment, each type of sugar might get you a different flavour.
